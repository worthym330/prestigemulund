Prestige City Forest Hills Mulund West

We are thrilled to introduce The Prestige City, a luxurious residential sanctuary designed for your ultimate enjoyment. Spanning 3.5 acres, this development features 1.8 acres of open-air spaces on the E-Deck, an opulent five-level clubhouse, and three inviting swimming pools. Engage in a range of sports, including Futsal, Badminton, and Squash, or enjoy entertainment at the Open Air Amphitheatre. Find tranquility in lush gardens, take advantage of the cycling track for rejuvenating fresh air, and experience Central Mumbai’s most expansive integrated hillside living, spread across 34 acres.
